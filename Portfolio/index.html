TourARound

TourARound stands as a pioneering Android Kotlin project aimed at elevating location-based experiences through augmented reality (AR) technology. Developed with precision and innovation, this groundbreaking initiative redefines how users interact with their surroundings. The project, constructed with meticulous detail, encompasses a range of features designed to enhance location-based interactions, offering a seamless and engaging user experience.

Within the broader scope of TourARound, my focused contribution revolves around critical aspects of the application. Primarily centered on location retrieval, seamless navigation, integrated emergency contact features, and augmented reality views, my work ensures that users can effortlessly explore nearby open locales. By implementing efficient direction assistance from their current position, I aim to provide a user-friendly and reliable experience. Furthermore, I have enriched TourARound with additional functionalities such as translation and object detection, pushing the boundaries of how individuals engage with and perceive their surroundings. Through my dedicated efforts, TourARound is poised to set new standards in the realm of location-based mobile applications.

TicketEase

With a passion for redefining international shipping dynamics, my efforts contribute to the project's mission of elevating the standards of cross-border logistics in the realm of peer-to-peer parcel delivery.
